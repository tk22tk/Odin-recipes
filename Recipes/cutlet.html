Chicken Cutlet

Delicious chicken cutlet for every weekend party.



Ingredients
  • 1kg chicken mince
  • 1 cup of finely chopped ginger and garlic
  • Few chopped green chillies
  • Handful of chopped mint and coriander leaves
  • Red chilli powder according to your spice level 1-2 spoons
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 eggs (beat with salt and black pepper)
  • 1 bowl of breadcrumbs
  • 2 cup cooking oil for frying


Steps
  1. Grind chicken mince slightly
  2. Add spices, herbs, green chillies & chopped ginger and garlic to the chicken, and mix really well.
  3. Take 2 tbsp of chicken mixture and make diamond shape.
  4. Coat all the chicken into bread crumbs one by one.
  5. Then dip them into beaten eggs and again into breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat some oil in the wok and deep fry the cutlets one by one.
  7. Keep flipping and cook from both sides.
  8. Once they turned golden brown, take them out on a kitchen tissue paper to absorb excess oil.

    9. Serve hot with ketchup or garlic dip.
