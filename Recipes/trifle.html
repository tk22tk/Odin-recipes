Fruit Trifle



An amazingly refreshing cold dessert made with fruits, whipped cream and sponge cake slices.

Ingredients
  • Fresh fruits:
    Kiwi, Pineapple, Melon, Grapes, Apple (or any of your favorites)
  • 2-3 cups Chilled Whipped Cream
  • 1.5 cups Powdered Sugar
  • chocolate or vanilla sponge cake (plain)
  • Nuts (optional)


Steps
  1. Finely dice all the fruits in a bowl.
  2. Beat the chilled whipped cream with sugar for 5-7 minutes or till it thicken.
  3. Chopped the cake slices into little squares like croutons.

Assembling
  1. In a big bowl make a first layer of cake pieces
  2. Now put some chopped mixed fruits and nuts
  3. Lastly add spoonful of whipped cream
  4. Keep repeating the above steps till you are done with all of your ingredients.
  5. Garnish with leftover cake crumbles and chocolate sauce/syrup
  6. Put the trifle bowl in the refrigerator to set

    7. Serve Cold !! Enjoy :)
