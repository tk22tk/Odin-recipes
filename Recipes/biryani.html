Chicken Biryani

Yummy and mouthwatering rice cooked in spices with chicken or meat of your choice.
One can always skip the meat and replace it with veggies



Ingredients
  • 1 kg chicken
  • 3 cups rice
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • few chopped green chillies
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • handful of chopped mint and coriander leaves
  • red chilli powder according to your spice level 1-2 spoons
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • cardamom pods
  • 1 cup cooking oil
  • Garnish: boiled eggs, fried potatoes and mint leaves (optional)


Steps
  1. Wash thoroughly chicken pieces and pat dry
  2. Marinade chicken pieces into yogurt and other ingredients except rice and cardamom pods
  3. Boil rice till one third with cardamom pod and a spoonful of salt
  4. strain the rice and keep aside
  5. Now in a big vessel add marinated chicken and put half done rice over it
  6. Tightly close the lid of the vessel, so that steam doesn't came out
  7. Cook on high flame for 5-7 minutes than lower the flame and cook biryani for almost 45 minutes
  8. Check in between if rice and chicken pieces are done
  9. cook till chicken pieces are tender
  10. Garnish with eggs, fries and mint leaves

    11. Serve hot with yogurt or chutney.
    Enjoy !!
