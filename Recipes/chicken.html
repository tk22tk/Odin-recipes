Butter Chicken



A succulent Butter chicken to enjoy on any occasion with Butter naan or boiled rice


Ingredients
  • 1 kg chicken
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 chopped onion
  • few chopped green chillies
  • handful of almonds and cashew nuts (optional)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • handful of chopped mint and coriander leaves
  • red chilli powder according to your spice level 1-2 spoons
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1-2 cup cooking oil
  • one stick of butter
  • fresh cream


Steps
  1. Wash thoroughly chicken pieces and pat dry
  2. Marinade chicken pieces into yogurt, salt, red chilli powder and ginger garlic paste
  3. Now in a deep wok, pour some oil and add marinated chicken and saute it for 5-7 minutes
  4. Remove the chicken pieces and add onion, tomatoes, green chillies, nuts.
  5. Saute on high flame for 5-7 minutes than add yogurt, cook for few minutes and keep aside to cool down
  6. Once cooled, grind the mixture into a paste
  7. In the same wok add some butter & oil and add in the chicken pieces with cardamom pods
  8. Add in the gravy in the chicken and cook till chicken pieces are tender
  9. Garnish with fresh cream and mint leaves

    10. Serve hot with Naan.
    Enjoy !!
