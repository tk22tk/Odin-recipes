Potato Bites



A heart warming light snack to have between meals or serve as a starter.


Ingredients
  • 3 medium potatoes - boiled
  • 1 chopped onion
  • few chopped green chillies
  • handful of chopped mint and coriander leaves
  • red chilli powder according to your spice level 1-2 spoons
  • salt to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp fresh cream
  • 1 bowl of breadcrumbs
  • 2 cup cooking oil for frying


Steps
  1. Mash boiled potatoes
  2. Add spices, herbs, onion, green chillies & fresh cream to the mashed potatoes, and mix really well.
  3. Make small cylindrical shape of the potato mixture.
  4. Coat all the potato bite into bread crumbs one by one.
  5. Heat some oil in the wok and deep fry the bites.
  6. Once they turned golden brown, take them out on a kitchen tissue paper to absorb excess oil.

    7. Serve hot with ketchup or garlic dip.
    Enjoy !!
